Privacy Policy

Nothing is Collected

The app collects no data, nor sends it to any 3rd party trackers. Apple may retain crash logs for me if something goes wrong, but that won't expose anything personal anyways.

The app stores some data locally on your device only, like the currencies you used last, and the colours of the gradients. That's it. Those don't leave the device either.

The only network request is to fetch the exchange rate desired. It's a "GET" request that only sends the "from" currency, and the "to" currency.
